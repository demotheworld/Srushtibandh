Our Story

Weaving Livelihoods from Nature's Gift

Founded by Sunil & Nirupama Deshpande to transform lives through bamboo artistry

Re-establishing Respect & Honour for Artisans

Srushtibandh started in the mid-90s from the tribal village of Lawada in Maharashtra's Melghat Forest. What began as an initiative to solve malnutrition problems has grown into a movement that empowers SC/ST communities through sustainable bamboo-based employment.
